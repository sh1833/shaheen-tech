FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How much does a project typically cost?+
Pricing depends on scope, but as a guide: landing pages from £500, full web apps from £2,000, e-commerce stores from £1,500, and mobile apps from £3,500. Every project gets a fixed-price proposal upfront — no hourly billing surprises.
How long does a project take?+
A landing page or simple site: 1–2 weeks. A full web app or e-commerce build: 4–8 weeks. Mobile apps: 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a firm timeline in the proposal and stick to it.
Do you work with clients outside of Coventry?+
Absolutely — the majority of our clients are fully remote across the UK. We work async over email and video calls, and you'll always know what's happening with your project.
What do you need from me to get started?+
Just a rough brief — what you're building, who it's for, and what your budget and timeline look like. You don't need anything polished. We'll help shape the project from there.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?+
Yes — we offer monthly maintenance retainers covering updates, security patches, hosting management, and ongoing feature development. Retainers start from £200/month.
Will I own everything when it's done?+
Yes. On final payment, all code, designs, and assets are fully yours. We hand over all source files, repo access, and credentials. No lock-in, ever.
