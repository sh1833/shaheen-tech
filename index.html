FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started with Shaheen Tech.

How much does a project typically cost?+
Every project is different, and so is the investment. We scope every project individually based on your requirements, goals, and timeline — ensuring you only pay for exactly what you need, nothing more. To get a clear, no-obligation quote tailored to your project, simply get in touch and we'll come back to you within 24 hours.
How long does a project take?+
We build around your timeline — not ours. Whether you're working to a tight launch date, an investor deadline, or a longer strategic rollout, we structure the project to fit. We are committed to delivering every project on time and ahead of schedule where possible. Deadlines are a commitment, not a suggestion.
Do you work with clients outside of Canterbury?+
Absolutely — we work with clients remotely across the whole of the UK. Everything is handled over email and video calls, and you'll always know exactly what's happening with your project.
What do you need from me to get started?+
Just a rough brief — what you're building, who it's for, and what your budget and timeline look like. You don't need anything polished. We'll help shape the project from there.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?+
Yes — we offer monthly maintenance retainers covering updates, security patches, hosting management, and ongoing feature development. Retainers start from £200/month.
Will I own everything when it's done?+
Yes. On final payment, all code, designs, and assets are fully yours. We hand over all source files, repo access, and credentials. No lock-in, ever.
Do you sign NDAs and contracts?+
Yes — every project starts with a clear contract that protects both parties. We're happy to sign NDAs before any sensitive discussions. Professionalism and trust are core to how we operate.
Can you work with an existing codebase?+
Yes. We regularly take on projects that involve working with or improving an existing codebase. We'll review the code first and give you an honest assessment before committing.

Still have questions? Just ask — we're happy to chat.
